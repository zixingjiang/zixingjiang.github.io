Welcome to my homepage! My name is Zixing. I'm a M.Phil. student at the [Department of Surgery, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)](https://www.surgery.cuhk.edu.hk/), working on medical and surgical robotics under the supervision of [Prof. LI Zheng](https://www.surgery.cuhk.edu.hk/profile.asp?alias=zli). My research focuses on studying and modeling medical procedures along with introducing computer-integrated technologies such as robotics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence to improve their the quality, efficiency, safety, as well as availability.

Prior to CUHK, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from [CUHK-Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en) in 2023, during which I spent three wonderful years in the [Robotics & Artificial Intelligence Laboratory](https://rail.cuhk.edu.cn/) under the mentorship of [Prof. QIAN Huihuan](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/qianhuihuan) and [Prof. SUN Zhenglong](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/sunzhenglong).
