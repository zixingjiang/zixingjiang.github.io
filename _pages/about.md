Welcome to my homepage! I'm a research assistant at the [Department of Surgery, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)](https://www.surgery.cuhk.edu.hk/), working under [Prof. LI Zheng](https://www.surgery.cuhk.edu.hk/profile.asp?alias=zli).  

I obtained my B.Eng. in Electronic Information Engineering from [CUHK-Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en) in the summer of 2023. During my undergraduate study, I spent roughly three years learning at the [Robotics & AI Lab](https://rail.cuhk.edu.cn/) under the supervision of [Prof. SUN Zhenglong](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/sunzhenglong) and [Prof. QIAN Huihuan](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/qianhuihuan).

In my spare time, I enjoy bikepacking and jogging. I'm a enthusiastic but casual RTS and shooter gamer. I'm also a fan of [Manchester City F.C.](https://www.mancity.com/) and [RedBull Racing](https://www.redbullracing.com/int-en).

# Research Interests

My research interests lie in achieving safe, stable, efficient, innovative, minimally invasive surgery, rehabilitation, and other medical procedures with the help of advanced engineering technologies such as robotics, artificial intelligence, computer vision, force sensing, etc. Additionally, I am interested in finding inspiration from nature creatures to optimize my robotic systems.

**Keywords:** Robot-assisted Medical Procedures, Image- and Force-guided Robotic System, Bio-inspired Robotics.
