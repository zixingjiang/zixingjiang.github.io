Welcome to my homepage! My name is Zixing Jiang. I completed my undergraduate studies at the [School of Science and Engineering](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en) (SSE), [The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en) (CUHK-Shenzhen) in the summer of 2023, with a specialization in electronics and information engineering. Currently (March. 2023 - present) I'm serving as a research assistant in CUHK-Shenzhen [Robotics & AI Lab](https://rail.cuhk.edu.cn/) (RAIL), working on [robot-assisted ultrasound](/projects/robot_assisted_ultrasound_examination/) under the supervision of [Prof. Zhenglong Sun](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/sunzhenglong). Prior to this (Sept. 2020 - Feb. 2023),  I was working on marine robotics under the guidance of [Prof. Huihuan Alex Qian](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/qianhuihuan).
