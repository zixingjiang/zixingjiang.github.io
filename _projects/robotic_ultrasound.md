## Introduction

Ultrasound examination is a cornerstone of modern noninvasive medical diagnostics, but it relies heavily on the techniques of clinicians and sonographers. This project aims to utilize robotics to automate and standardize the ultrasound examination process to avoid misdiagnosis and release doctors from routine procedures so that they can focus on top-level decision-making.
